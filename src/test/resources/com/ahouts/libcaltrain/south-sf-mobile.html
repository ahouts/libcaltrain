South San Francisco Station Parking Impacts

Jan. 8, 2018 until TBD

Starting January 8, 2018, parking spaces #13 to 23 will be blocked off to set up the staging area for the South San Francisco Improvement Project.

Due to this work, parking will be limited at the South San Francisco Station. There will be 29 parking spaces available plus two ADA spaces. Customers are advised to adjust their commute accordingly.

Please board and alight train from new temporary platform.
